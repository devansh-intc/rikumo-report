This is the #1 question to answer on the client call.

Orders are nearly identical (417 in 2025 vs 411 in 2026 — only 1.4% difference). The revenue gap is driven by two factors: a lower Average Order Value and a missing promotional event.

8. Recommendations & Action Plan

1. Launch a Flash Sale / Promotion (March 19–22)
Last year’s March 12 promo generated $13,252.52 in a single day with 89 orders. Run a 48–72hr flash sale (20–25% off best sellers) promoted via Klaviyo email blast + social. This is the single biggest lever to close the gap. Target: $8,000–15,000 in incremental sales.
2. Promote High-AOV Products (Samplers, Kits & Bundles)
The AOV drop is the core revenue issue. Push high-AOV products in the flash sale and email campaigns:
  • Vegetable Sampler (15 ZIP) — #1 seller at $4,585.60
  • Deluxe Sampler (32 ZIP), Pantry Stuffers, Family Packs — all $100+ AOV products
  • Create a “Best Value Bundles” featured collection on the homepage
Goal: bring AOV back toward $181.57 (from current $162.14).
3. Scale Google PMax Budget ($190/day → $250–300/day)
PMax is budget-limited at 4.88x ROAS — this is the highest-efficiency paid channel. Increasing budget should capture incremental Shopping traffic. Branded Search (6.85x ROAS) should remain as-is. Estimated incremental: $60–110/day extra spend × ~4x ROAS = $240–440/day additional revenue.
4. Increase Email Frequency (Klaviyo)
Email is driving 21 orders / $3,875.77 in total sales at zero ad cost (infinite ROI). Send 2–3 additional blasts this week: flash sale announcement, abandoned cart recovery push, “best sellers of March” curated collection. Each blast can drive $500–2,000 in incremental sales.
5. Optimize Facebook for Higher-AOV Products
Facebook is driving 100 orders at 2.26x UTM ROAS (4.92x platform). Focus ad creatives on high-AOV products (samplers, kits, bundles) rather than single-item products. This can help lift both Facebook ROAS and overall AOV simultaneously.
6. Set Realistic Expectations on 15% Target
The 15% growth target ($134,553.51) needs $5,761.63/day for the remaining 14 days — that’s 1.8x the current daily average of $3,170.04. A flash sale + optimizations can get us closer, but matching last year’s flash sale performance is the key unlock. A realistic target: match or come within 5–10% of last year’s $117,003.05.
